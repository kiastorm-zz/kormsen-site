
This module provides color classes

```html
<p class="white-washout">white washout</p>
<p class="light-washout">light washout</p>
<p class="washout">dark washout</p>
<p class="dark-washout">dark washout</p>

<p class="extra-light-storm">extra light storm</p>
<p class="light-storm">light storm</p>
<p class="storm">storm</p>
<p class="light-tarmac">dark storm</p>

<p class="extra-light-tarmac">extra light tarmac</p>
<p class="light-tarmac">light tarmac</p>
<p class="tarmac">tarmac</p>
<p class="dark-tarmac">dark tarmac</p>

<p class="extra-light-sky-blue">extra light sky blue</p>
<p class="light-sky-blue">light sky blue</p>
<p class="sky-blue">sky blue</p>
<p class="dark-sky-blue">dark sky blue</p>

<p class="extra-light-turquoise">extra light turquoise</p>
<p class="light-turquoise">light turquoise</p>
<p class="turquoise">turquoise</p>
<p class="dark-turquoise">dark turquoise</p>

<p class="orange">orange</p>
<p class="color-inherit">reset color</p>
```
